How It Works

This demo uses Sentence Transformers to power semantic search:

  1. Your search query is converted into a numerical vector using a pre-trained model
  2. This vector captures the semantic meaning of your query
  3. The system compares your query vector with pre-computed product vectors
  4. Products are ranked by similarity score (higher = more relevant)

Unlike traditional keyword search, this can understand concepts and find relevant matches even when exact words don't appear in the product descriptions.
